How to Use the POS Terminal

  1. Navigate to the POS Terminal from the dashboard.
  2. Click on product cards on the left to add them to the current order.
  3. Use the `+` and `-` buttons in the cart to adjust quantities.
  4. If the customer has a promo code, enter it and click "Apply".
  5. Select the payment method from the dropdown menu.
  6. Click "Complete Sale" to finalize the order. The cart will automatically clear for the next customer.

How to Track Orders

  1. Navigate to the Track Orders page.
  2. The page shows all recent orders, with the newest at the top.
  3. To update an order's status, simply select a new status from the dropdown (e.g., from "Pending" to "In Progress").
  4. If an order is "Cancelled", the items from that order will be automatically added back to the product inventory.

How to Manage Staff

  1. Navigate to the Manage Staff page.
  2. Click "Add New Staff" to open a form and create a new user. You must assign them a role and a temporary password.
  3. Click the "Edit" button to change a user's details or role.
  4. Click the "Delete" button to permanently remove a user from the system.

How to Use the Daily Checklist

  1. All employees should visit the Daily Checklist page during their shift.
  2. Review the list of required health and safety tasks.
  3. After completing a task, click the "Log Task" button next to it.
